# Studio Dashboard: Strategic Feature Plan

## Core Objective
Incentivize Studio Owners to claim their studio profile, self-report missing awards from unscraped local competitions, and actively manage their dancer rosters. The platform should become their ultimate marketing and analytical tool.

## Feature Ideas

### 1. The Ultimate Marketing "Trophy Room"
- **Embeddable Studio Widgets:** Provide a snippet of code (iframe) that studios can paste into their own Wix/Wordpress websites to display their dynamic "Trophy Room" or "Alumni Hall of Fame" directly powered by our database. This saves them hours of web development.
- **Public Prestige Page:** A premium, dark-mode public profile that acts as the studio's ultimate recruiting tool for prospective parents, highlighting national titles and lifetime achievement metrics.

### 2. Choreography & Routine Analytics
- **Win-Rate by Choreographer:** Allow studios to tag routines with specific choreographers and visualize which choreographers are yielding the highest ROI (Highest Adjudications, Most Overalls).
- **Competition Benchmarking:** Analytics that show how the studio performs at different circuits (e.g., "Your win rate is 15% higher at NYCDA than at KAR").

### 3. Self-Reporting & Roster Management
- **Local Competition Uploads:** A simple CSV/Excel uploader allowing them to input results from smaller competitions we don't scrape. These awards are marked as "Self-Reported" until verified.
- **Roster & Graduation Tracking:** An interface to manage their current roster of dancers. When dancers graduate, they are moved to the "Alumni" section, building the studio's legacy.

### 4. Dancer Portfolios for College/Agency Recruiting
- **Resume Exports:** A feature allowing studio owners to generate beautifully formatted "Dance Resumes" for their senior dancers. The resume automatically pulls all lifetime 1st place finishes, titles, and scholarships from the database.

### 5. Financial / Scholarship Tracking
- **Scholarship Vault:** A private tracker showing the total dollar amount of scholarships won by the studio's dancers over the season, providing a tangible ROI metric the studio can pitch to parents.
