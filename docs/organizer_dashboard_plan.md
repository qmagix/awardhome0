# Organizer Dashboard: Strategic Feature Plan

## Core Objective
Incentivize Competition Organizers (e.g., NYCDA, KAR, YAGP) to stop relying on PDF uploads and instead push their results directly via API or CSV/Excel upload to our platform. By doing so, we save scraping costs and ensure 100% data integrity, while providing them with a premium SaaS-like experience.

More thoughts from Gemini 

### 1. The "Verified" Prestige Hook
- **Instant Dancer Notifications:** When an organizer uploads results directly, the awards receive an official "Verified Organizer" badge. Dancers are instantly notified via email/SMS, creating immediate social media buzz for the competition.
- **Social Sharing Kits:** Auto-generate beautifully branded Instagram/TikTok graphics for the top overall winners that dancers and studios can share, embedding the organizer's watermark. 

### 2. Deep Analytics & Demographics [Later, also data only partial, since not all attending studio have awards, so those not in our database]
- **Studio Retention Metrics:** A visual dashboard showing YoY retention. *"75% of studios from Baltimore 2025 returned in 2026."*
- **Geographic Heatmaps:** Visual maps showing where their attending studios are traveling from, helping organizers identify untapped regional markets for future tour stops.
- **Registration Trends:** Cross-reference award data with attendance size to see which age divisions or categories are growing or shrinking (e.g., "Tap routines are up 20% this year").

### 3. Competitor Intelligence & Benchmarking [Also later, limited by our data]
- **Cross-Platform Analytics:** Show organizers how their event sizing compares to regional averages. (e.g., "Your Teen Solo division is 30% larger than the national average").

### 4. Direct Registration Pipeline [put a checkbox item about this on orga dashboard]]
- **Seamless Upselling:** A "Register for Next Year" button directly embedded on their verified historical results page, capturing dancers and studio owners while they are riding the high of viewing their awards.

### 5. Secure Score & Critique Distribution (Premiumm, later stage for paying organizers)
- **Private Access Control:** Allow organizers to upload judge critiques (audio/video) alongside the results. Studio owners can log in to securely download them, forcing studio owners to adopt the platform and cementing our app as the central hub of the industry.
